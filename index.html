About Skull&Bones

@Star ATLAS

Skull & Bones is Star Atlas’ 1st and largest piracy DAC (decentralized autonomous corporation)

Our roots are based on the philosophies of the pirates of old. We are a band of the wild ones, free thinkers, fighters, oddballs, nerds, oldies and youngin’s- all on the quest for ultimate Freedom. At our core we are a crew of player vs. players, however; we welcome all those with the heart and yearning for the pirate life. Skull & Bones has roles and room for all.
